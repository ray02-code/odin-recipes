NY Strip

Description

This amazing NY strip is going to show the value in one of the cheaper cuts of steaks, and explode with flavor!

Ingridients

  • New York strip Steak
  • salt
  • thyme
  • 4 tbs unsalted Butter
  • 1 garlic clove

Steps

  1. pour a high smoke point oil into a pan with heat on high
  2. dry off steak and salt
  3. when oil starts to smoke place steak on thin side in the pan
  4. sear everyside for 1 1/2 minutes moving steak in the pan for a even sear
  5. when all sides are seared reduce heat to medium low and throw in the butter and thyme
  6. baste the steaks with the butter and thyme for around 6 minutes or until desired internal temperature is 10 degrees less
  7. lastly let the steak sit and cook on your plate for 10 minutes then serve and enjoy
