Scrambled Eggs

Description

Quick and easy breakfest for the family that doesnt loose out on flavor for speed.

Ingridients

  • 4 Eggs
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • butter
  • green onion

Steps

  1. turn heat to medium
  2. whisk eggs in a bowl
  3. pour eggs into small sauce pan while constantly stirring
  4. add cream when eggs begin to thicken
  5. stir until desired consistency
  6. garnish with green onion and other seasonings including butter
